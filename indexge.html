How We Work?

  • Buying the car at auction in the United States (includes both the provision of keys to auctions for dealers and/or purchasing the car by one of our managers)
  • Ground transportation of the car to the nearest port in the United States
  • Photo report from the terminal
  • Consolidation of cargo
  • Loading the car in a container for sea freight
  • Arrival of the container in the port of Odessa
  • Forwarding and customs clearance
  • Transfer of the car to the final buyer, as well as its delivery to any point in Ukraine
