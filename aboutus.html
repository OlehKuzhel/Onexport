Our story

We would like to start our relationship with the truth because there is nothing in good relationship with truth and good faith (including business relationships) The idea of the creation of the Company ON Export emerged in 2018, in Ukraine, in particular, in Kyiv by the common initiative of the youth working in the field.

In a short time, with the help of our friends, we registered "our family" (yes we call this company ) in New York City in the USA.

We received a dealer license and started business activities. The first steps were made in Georgia and Ukraine, as far as the hottest places for that time were in the above-mentioned countries.

Soon we found our confederates and our initiative was carried out. As we are today, we continue to work on fast pace and protect our fundamental principles strictly, to be transparent, professionally attitude towards the case and not only in the care format fo r our interests. It's your valuing to determine our work!

Despite the fact that there is a big competition in terms of transportation and sale of motor transport in this field as in the United States as other countries (Georgia, Armenia, Poland, Africa, Emirates, Azerbaijan, Ukraine, and Kazakhstan), we had difficulties to find a company that meets modern standards in terms of quality and price. Also the company's actions - that will be transparent and tailored to the customer's interests despite the difficulties in the field. To simplify, we have imagined our heads at the customer's place and for unsuccessful results we took that step.
